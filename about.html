Artists of the factory

as a passionate musician who enjoys playing with many different people, i have accumulated quite a bit of music, but have worked with many different groups of musicians. i find it tedious and unorderly to try and maintain several different websites, myspaces, etc. thus, i created this pickle factory, a common space to share all of my music, as well as songs from close friends. all music in the pickle factory is free from restrictions; do as you please with these numbers. my hope is that this website will inspire musical innovation and collaboration amongst other musicians.

please, please, please send me an email at benjamin.j.wagner.1@gmail.com if you would like to be part of the factory! heck, send me an email just to say hello or share some constructive criticism. i also want to stress my appreciation for suggestions and future ideas for the pickle factory.
