Welcome to the pickle factory

Posted by Benjamin Wagner on 09 April 2009  • 

welcome to the pickle factory. as a passionate musician, i often become frustrated with the mass of different music-sharing mediums. therefore, i have created this website simply to share and inspire musical innovations. my intent is to encourage collaboration and inspiration to other musicians. in doing this, i propose to place less emphasis on the names and labels which we assign ourselves as musicians. as a musician who enjoys playing with many different people, i wanted a common space to share all of my songs - as well as sounds from fellow musicians.


the future of the pickle factory is indefinite and certainly open to new ideas. please email me (benjamin.j.wagner.1@gmail.com) with any ideas or if you would like to be part of our community.
