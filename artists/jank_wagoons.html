Jank Wagoons (Jankwagoons)

during the severe panic of the incoming millenium, a group of young, good-looking guys had the idea to create a band that would bring humor and immaturity to the ears of many. this humor and immaturity consisted of anything from fart jokes to having fun with some new-age artists. the jankwagoons were in the spotlight due to their real joy in life, that which was music. they are a band that is one of few who don't care about the big music scene and don't plan on getting into the eye of the camera. with such this attitude, the jankwagoons will always be here to be the music genius' they are.

the jank wagoons are all on vacation for a while.
